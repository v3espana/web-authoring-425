Bridging the Gap Between Beginners and Climbers

Starting out in climbing can feel overwhelming. There's too much gear, too many opinions, and not always clear advice. That's where we come in! The Crux Corner is a space built for people who are new, interested, and even the least bit curious about climbing. Whether you are choosing your first pair of shoes, working on technique, or building confidence on the wall, you are in the right place.

Climbing is challenging enough, but getting into it shouldn't be! Here, you'll find beginner-friendly advice, honest experiences, and guidance from fellow climbing peers. No pressure, no judgement, just a place to learn, improve, and enjoy our climbing journeys.
